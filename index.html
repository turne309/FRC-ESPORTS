Highly Competitive Racecraft

Reliable Scheduling

Drive on the limit with scheduled practices, qualifying, races and other events every week to hone in on your skills and get your adrenaline fix.

Tight Community

With thousands of members across all platforms, it can be hard to find a spot to settle in. To mitigate this, we have created numerous channels, teams and servers to sort members into specific areas based on a vast number of considerations. If the spot isn’t right, a move is as easy as a pen stroke.

Dedicated Staff

FRC has a plethora of staff members all with unique roles ranging from recruitment, event planning, stewarding, live streaming and much more. Our staff provides a professional level experience at every step of the way.

Advanced Ranking

Each series has a thoroughly calculated system in place to determine driver skill, recruit drivers, award penalties and move drivers within divisions to ensure every single event is as competitive as possible.

How To Start?

Sign up for a free FRC account.

Make sure you have Discord and F1 downloaded on your platform of choice.

Accept the server invitation link that is sent to you.

Watch the welcome guide and begin your career!

About Us

FRC is a community founded by sim racing enthusiasts that strive to bring a professional level experience to its members by dedicating countless hours to organizing the fairest and most competitive racing environment possible. All of our staff volunteer there hours out of pure love for the game. We are always looking to expand and bring our experiences to a larger audience. We hope our work is appreciated by our audience and members and thank everyone for their unwavering support.
